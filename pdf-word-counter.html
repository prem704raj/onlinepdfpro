    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q. Does this count words page by page?

    Yes. You get total word count plus per-page breakdown for detailed review.

    Q. Will scanned image PDFs be counted correctly?

    Scanned PDFs with no selectable text may return low counts. Text-based PDFs give best results.

    Q. Is there a file size limit?

    There is no server-side limit. Performance depends on your browser memory and device capacity.

    Q. Is my PDF uploaded to any server?

    No. Processing runs fully in your browser and files stay on your device.

    Q. Can I copy and export the statistics?

    Yes. Use Copy Stats or Export TXT to save your analysis quickly.
