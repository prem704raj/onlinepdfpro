    Q. How do I split a PDF into separate pages?

    Upload your PDF, select the pages you want to extract, and click 'Split.' Each selected page or range is saved as a separate PDF that you can download.

    Q. Can I extract specific page ranges from a PDF?

    Yes! You can select individual pages, ranges (e.g., pages 3-7), or multiple non-consecutive pages to extract from your PDF.

    Q. Is splitting PDFs free with no signup?

    Absolutely. OnlinePDFPro's PDF splitter is 100% free with no account required. Your PDF is processed locally in your browser.

    Q. Does it work on mobile phones?

    Yes! OnlinePDFPro works perfectly on all devices - Android phones, iPhones, tablets, laptops and desktops. No app download needed.
