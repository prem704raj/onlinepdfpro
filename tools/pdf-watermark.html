    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q. How to add a watermark to a PDF?

    Upload your PDF, type your watermark text (e.g., 'CONFIDENTIAL', 'DRAFT') or upload an image, adjust position and opacity, then download the watermarked PDF.

    Q. Can I use an image as a watermark?

    Yes! Upload a logo, stamp, or any image as a watermark. You can adjust its size, opacity, and position on the page.

    Q. Will the watermark appear on every page?

    Yes, by default the watermark is applied to all pages. You can also choose to watermark only specific pages.

    Q. Does it work on mobile phones?

    Yes! OnlinePDFPro works perfectly on all devices - Android phones, iPhones, tablets, laptops and desktops. No app download needed.

    Q. Is there a file size limit?

    Since processing happens in your browser, there's no server-side file size limit. You can process files as large as your device can handle.
