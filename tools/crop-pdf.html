    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q. How to crop a PDF online?

    Upload your PDF, draw a crop area on the page or enter exact dimensions, then apply the crop. Download your trimmed PDF instantly.

    Q. Can I crop all pages at once?

    Yes! Apply the same crop area to all pages in your PDF, or crop each page individually for precise control.

    Q. Does cropping reduce file size?

    Yes, cropping removes content outside the crop area, which can reduce the PDF file size.

    Q. Does it work on mobile phones?

    Yes! OnlinePDFPro works perfectly on all devices - Android phones, iPhones, tablets, laptops and desktops. No app download needed.

    Q. Is there a file size limit?

    Since processing happens in your browser, there's no server-side file size limit. You can process files as large as your device can handle.
