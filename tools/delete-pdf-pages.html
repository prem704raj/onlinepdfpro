    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q. How do I delete specific pages from a PDF for free?

    Upload your PDF to OnlinePDFPro, select the pages you want to remove by clicking on them, then click 'Delete Selected.' Download your trimmed PDF instantly — no signup required.

    Q. Can I preview pages before deleting them?

    Yes! Each page is displayed as a thumbnail preview so you can clearly see which pages to remove before making any changes.

    Q. Will deleting pages reduce my PDF file size?

    Yes. Removing pages also removes their content, which directly reduces the file size of your final PDF document.

    Q. Does it work on mobile phones?

    Yes! OnlinePDFPro works perfectly on all devices - Android phones, iPhones, tablets, laptops and desktops. No app download needed.

    Q. Is there a file size limit?

    Since processing happens in your browser, there's no server-side file size limit. You can process files as large as your device can handle.
