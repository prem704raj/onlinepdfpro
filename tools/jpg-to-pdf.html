    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q. How to convert images to PDF?

    Upload your JPG, PNG, or WEBP images, drag to reorder them, choose page settings, then download the combined PDF. All pages are included in one document.

    Q. Can I adjust page size and margins?

    Yes! Choose between A4, Letter, or auto-fit page sizes. You can also adjust margins and orientation for each page.

    Q. How many images can I combine?

    There's no limit on the number of images. Combine as many as your device can handle — all processing is local.

    Q. Does it work on mobile phones?

    Yes! OnlinePDFPro works perfectly on all devices - Android phones, iPhones, tablets, laptops and desktops. No app download needed.

    Q. Is there a file size limit?

    Since processing happens in your browser, there's no server-side file size limit. You can process files as large as your device can handle.
