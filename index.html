    📖 Why I Built This

    I used to spend way too much time jumping between different websites just to get basic PDF work done — one site for merging, another for compressing, a third one for converting. And half of them had daily limits, forced sign-ups, or watermarked the output unless you paid.

    It was frustrating. I just wanted one place where I could do everything — merge, split, compress, convert, sign, add watermarks, extract text — without switching tabs or hitting some random paywall after two files.

    So I built OnlinePDFPro for myself. 30+ tools, all in one place, no limits, no sign-ups. Every tool runs right in your browser — fast, simple, and completely free.

    I made it for my own daily use, but if it helps you too — go ahead and use it. That's exactly why it's here.
