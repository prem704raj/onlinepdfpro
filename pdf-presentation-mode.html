    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q. Can I control slides with keyboard keys?

    Yes. Arrow keys, spacebar, and other shortcuts are supported in presentation mode.

    Q. Does swipe navigation work on touch devices?

    Yes. Swipe left or right on phones and tablets to change slides.

    Q. Can I annotate while presenting?

    Yes. Turn on Draw mode to sketch annotations over current slides.

    Q. Is auto slideshow timing configurable?

    Yes. Use 5s, 10s, 30s presets or set a custom interval.

    Q. Is presentation processing offline and private?

    Yes. Slides are rendered in-browser and files are not uploaded to external servers.
