Step-by-Step Guide to Splitting Large PDF Files

Large PDF files can be a headache. They often exceed email attachment limits, take too long to load, and can be cumbersome to navigate if you only need a specific chapter or section. Splitting your PDF is the perfect solution.

Why Split a PDF?

  • Email Limits: Most email providers limit attachments to 25MB. Splitting helps you bypass this without losing data.
  • Extracting Relevance: If you only need a 5-page report out of a 200-page manual, splitting allows you to isolate and share just what's needed.
  • Faster Loading: Smaller files render instantly on mobile devices and slower internet connections.

How to Split a PDF Online

  2. Choose the specific page ranges you want to extract, or choose to split the document every X pages.
  3. Click 'Split PDF' and instantly download your new, smaller files.
