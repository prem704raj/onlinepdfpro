Why Convert Images to PDF?

  • Consolidation: Combine 20 individual photos into one single file, making it much easier to share over email or chat.
  • Standardization: Ensure your document can be opened and viewed exactly as intended on any device.
  • Protection: PDFs can be secured with passwords and encryption, providing a layer of security that raw images lack.
