Why Do PDF Files Get So Large?

PDFs are great because they look exactly the same on any device. However, this fidelity comes at a cost. The main culprits behind massive PDF files include:

  • High-Resolution Images: Unoptimized, high DPI images take up a massive amount of space.
  • Embedded Fonts: Some PDFs embed entire font families instead of just the characters used.
  • Hidden Data: Unnecessary metadata, old document revisions, and hidden layers can bloat file sizes.
