Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How does this free presentation maker work?

Our presentation maker runs entirely within your web browser using HTML5 Canvas. You can add slides, text, images, and backgrounds, and it all stays perfectly private on your device.

Q. Can I download my presentation as a PowerPoint file?

Yes! You can instantly export your creation as a standard .PPTX file compatible with Microsoft PowerPoint and Google Slides, or as a static PDF document.

Q. Do I need to sign up to create slides?

No, you can start creating presentations immediately without creating an account or paying for a subscription. There are absolutely no watermarks on your exported files.

Q. Will you store my presentation data?

No. The entire editing process is strictly client-side. We never store, read, or process your slides on our servers.
