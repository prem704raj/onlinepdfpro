Privacy and compatibility

The PDF Reader runs entirely securely, so the file stays on your device. It supports modern browsers on Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS with no account required.

Security notes

  • Files are processed locally in the tab session.
  • No uploads or server-side storage.
  • Close the tab to clear the session.

Compatibility

  • Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari supported.
  • Works on mobile and tablet browsers.
  • Large files load faster on newer devices.
