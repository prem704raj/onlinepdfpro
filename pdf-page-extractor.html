    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q. Can I extract non-contiguous pages?

    Yes. Select any combination of pages, including mixed ranges and single pages.

    Q. Is range input required to select pages?

    No. You can click thumbnails directly, then optionally refine selection with ranges.

    Q. What is the ZIP export option?

    ZIP mode creates one 4K PNG image per selected page and packages them together.

    Q. Is extracted output same quality as source?

    Yes. PDF export preserves document quality, and image export renders each page as a high-resolution 4K PNG.

    Q. Is this tool private?

    Yes. All extraction runs in-browser and your files are not sent to cloud servers.
